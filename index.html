Chicken

Chicken is a widely consumed poultry known for its versatility and lean protein. It's used in countless dishes, from roasted and grilled to soups and stir-fries, making it a staple in many cuisines around the world.

Beef

Beef, derived from cattle, is a rich and flavorful meat used in numerous dishes globally. It's known for its versatility, offering various cuts like steaks, roasts, and ground beef, each suited to different cooking methods.

Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice, often accompanied by raw fish, vegetables, and seaweed. It's celebrated for its delicate flavors, artistic presentation, and cultural significance.
